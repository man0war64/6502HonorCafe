Care Instructions

  • Water needs to be added regularly (take out the water tank in the back and fill it).
  • If the machine is out of beans, look inside the fridge for more beans and top up the bean container.
  • When the drip tray or grounds are full, empty them to ensure the machine continues to function properly.
  • Instructions: Jura A1-A100 Manual
  • Keep the machine and the counter clean!
